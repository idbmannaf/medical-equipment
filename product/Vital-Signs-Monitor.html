Vital Signs Monitor

About Product:

Products Full Description:

    CMS5100 Vital Signs Monitor Spo2/NIBP Monitor adopts Oscillometry for NIBP measuring, Photoelectric Oxyhemoglobin Inspection Technology combining Capacity Pulse Scanning & Recording Technology for SpO2 measuring. Systolic Pressure(SYS), Diastolic Pressure(DIA), Mean Pressure(MAP), SpO2 and Pulse rate(PR) can be accurately measured.

    Compact appearance, comprehensive functions, simple and convenient operation, which is applicable for hospitals, community medical treatment and family.

Performance:

  1. NIBP monitoring
  2. Measurement method: Oscillometry
  3. Resolution: 1mmHg
  4. Systolic pressure: 40mmHg~135mmHg
  5. Diastolic Pressure: 10mmHg~100mmHg
